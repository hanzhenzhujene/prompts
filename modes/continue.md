# Mode: Continue

For resuming agentic work mid-stream. Layers on `core.md` and (optionally) a task mode.

## Workflow

1. Review state. Read what exists. Infer what was completed.
2. Restate the goal. State the gap between current state and goal.
3. Pick the next step that closes the largest part of the gap.
4. State the rationale before acting.
5. Execute the planned step. Verify with the relevant test or check.
6. Report.

## Report format

1. Current progress
2. Next step chosen, and why over alternatives
3. What changed
4. Remaining risks or blockers

## Avoid

- Confirmation requests when not blocked by missing information or permissions
- Work outside the planned step (log sidequests as risks instead)
- Iterating past the goal. Stop once the goal is met.
- Optimizing the current step in isolation from the overall gap
